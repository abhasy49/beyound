Vision

To contribute to sustainable development across africa with capacity to engage the public policy systems of Africa states in the overall interest of the poor masses.

Mission

To be a leading voice in the advocacy for good governance towards building an egalitarian society.

Values

Leadership
Responsibility
Legacy
People Focus
Accountability
Equity and Justice

Aims and Objectives

  • To implant the tenets of democracy in the minds of the youth and promote good leadership through conferences, seminars, symposia,publications, awards and use of media.
  • To research, evaluate and monitor the impact of socio-economic policies of African States to ensure that African  States players formulate and drive sustainable policies for the betterment of citizens.
  • To provide scholarships and grants to brilliant children, women, youths and persons with perculiar challenges who are unable to go to school due to financial constraints.
  • To contribute to poverty alleviation amongst less priviledged persons in Nigeria and other African Countries by empowering them through enlightenement, welfare and capacity building programmes.
  • To advance unity, peace and development in Nigeria and Africa at large.

Our Current Projects

Scholarship Program

At BBLLI, we believe that to grow Africa, every child must have access to quality education. BBLLI has given eduactional support to 20 less privileged children across Nigeria.  We are currently sponsoring 10 students in different schools within Nasarawa State.

Youth Leadership Training

We have trained over 300 youths in rural communities to become better leaders. We are currently training youth leaders and community influencers in Keffi Local Government Area of Nasarawa State,

Support US

Almajiri Support Program

Almajiris are children whose parents or guardians have abandoned. Instead of leaving them to become nuisances, we  take care of them by providing food and other material care. About 100 Almajiris have benefited from our Almajiri Project.

Peace Advocacy

BBLLI has organized peace summits and other  peace advocacy programmes aimed at promoting peacful co-existence among citizens especially the youths.
